## Logging in

### Access the system

1. **Navigate to the CSP Rota Management System page:** [Login Page →](https://spitfire-staging.onrender.com/auth/login)
2. **Enter your credentials** - username and password provided during account setup
3. **Click "Sign-in"** to access the system
4. **Complete any additional verification** if required by your organization

## Troubleshooting login issues

### Common problems and solutions

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Invalid username or password">
    <p>
      <strong>Check credentials</strong>: Verify username and password are entered correctly<br/>
      <strong>Caps lock</strong>: Ensure caps lock is not accidentally enabled<br/>
      <strong>Account status</strong>: Contact administrator if account may be disabled<br/>
      <strong>Password reset</strong>: Use forgot password feature if needed
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Account locked or suspended">
    <p>
      <strong>Multiple failed attempts</strong>: Account may lock after repeated failed logins<br/>
      <strong>Security hold</strong>: Contact administrator for account unlock<br/>
      <strong>Policy violation</strong>: Review system usage policies and contact support<br/>
      <strong>Temporary suspension</strong>: Wait for lockout period to expire
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="System access issues">
    <p>
      <strong>Browser compatibility</strong>: Ensure using supported browser version<br/>
      <strong>Network connectivity</strong>: Check internet connection and firewall settings<br/>
      <strong>System maintenance</strong>: Check for scheduled maintenance notifications<br/>
      <strong>Cache/cookies</strong>: Clear browser cache and cookies if experiencing issues
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>

### Getting help

If you continue to experience login problems:

1. **Check system status** - Look for maintenance notifications or system alerts
2. **Contact your administrator** - They can verify account status and permissions
3. **Document the issue** - Note exact error messages and steps that led to the problem
4. **Try alternative access** - Use different browser or device if available

## Security best practices

### Protecting your account

- **Never share your login credentials** with anyone
- **Log out completely** when finished, especially on shared computers
- **Use strong, unique passwords** that aren't used for other systems
- **Report suspicious activity** immediately to system administrators
- **Keep personal information current** so security communications reach you

### Safe usage

- **Be aware of your surroundings** when accessing sensitive information
- **Lock your screen** when stepping away from your computer
- **Use secure networks** and avoid public Wi-Fi for system access
- **Keep your browser updated** with the latest security patches
