# Welcome to the Standard CSP Rota - Learning Materials


This documentation will help you understand and effectively use the Spitfire Rota Management System for operational coordination and workforce planning.

## What is the CSP Rota Management System?

The CSP Rota Management is a comprehensive operational management system designed for coordinating events, managing schedules, and tracking tasks across multiple teams and contracts. It provides the tools needed to:

- **Submit and track operational requests**
- **Manage team schedules and availability**
- **Coordinate event assignments and coverage**
- **Track progress on operational tasks**
- **Maintain team structure and user access**

## Who uses Rota Management System?

The system serves multiple operational roles, each with specific capabilities:

- <Badge text="Admin" variant="danger" /> **System Administrators** - Full system control and user management
- <Badge text="Operations Support" variant="caution" /> **Operations Support** - Request processing and operational coordination
- <Badge text="Operations Manager" variant="success" /> **Operations Managers** - Team management and schedule oversight
- <Badge text="COO" variant="tip" /> **Senior Management** - Strategic oversight and approval authority
- <Badge text="Operational Staff" variant="note" /> **Operational Staff** - Request submission and task execution
