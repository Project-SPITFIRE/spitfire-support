## What is a request?

A request is a formal submission for operational needs such as:
- Resource allocation (staff, equipment, budget)
- Process improvements or changes
- Schedule modifications
- Cross-team coordination
- Emergency operational needs

## Request statuses

### Active Statuses
- <Badge text="Draft" variant="note" /> **Draft**: Work in progress, not yet submitted
- <Badge text="Submitted" variant="caution" /> **Submitted**: Awaiting initial review
- <Badge text="Under Review" variant="tip" /> **Under Review**: Being evaluated by Operations Support
- <Badge text="Missing Information" variant="danger" /> **Missing Information**: Requires additional details
- <Badge text="In Progress" variant="caution" /> **In Progress**: Actively being processed
- <Badge text="On Hold" variant="note" /> **On Hold**: Temporarily paused pending external factors

### Final Statuses
- <Badge text="Completed" variant="success" /> **Completed**: Successfully fulfilled
- <Badge text="Rejected" variant="danger" /> **Rejected**: Declined with reasons provided
- <Badge text="Cancelled" variant="note" /> **Cancelled**: Withdrawn by submitter

## Required information

All requests must include:

**Basic Details**:
- Clear, descriptive title
- Detailed description of need
- Business justification
- Requested timeline

**Specifics**:
- Related contracts or events (if applicable)
- Resource requirements
- Impact if not fulfilled
- Alternative solutions considered

**Additional for Complex Requests**:
- Budget implications
- Cross-team dependencies
- Risk assessment
- Success criteria

## Best practices

**For Submitters**:
- Be specific and detailed in descriptions
- Include all relevant context and background
- Respond quickly to clarification requests
- Update timeline if priorities change

**For Reviewers**:
- Review requests within 24 hours
- Ask clarifying questions promptly
- Provide clear status updates
- Document decision rationale
