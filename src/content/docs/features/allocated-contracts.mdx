## What are Allocated Contracts?

Allocated Contracts are operational agreements and service contracts assigned to specific managers or teams for execution and oversight. These include:
- **Security & Stewarding contracts** for event safety
- **Traffic Management contracts** for transportation coordination
- **Client-specific contracts** with defined scopes and durations
- **Departmental assignments** with specific client codes

## Best Practices

:::tip[Contract Management Best Practices]
- **Regular Review:** Check contract details and requirements regularly
- **Team Coordination:** Ensure teams understand contract obligations
- **Client Communication:** Maintain regular contact with contract clients
- **Documentation:** Keep detailed records of contract execution and issues
- **Resource Planning:** Allocate appropriate resources for contract delivery
:::

:::note[Integration Points]
Allocated Contracts integrates with:
- **Team Management:** Assigning teams to specific contracts
- **Rota Management:** Scheduling staff for contract delivery
- **EOS Actions:** Contract-related tasks and coordination
- **Advanced Contracts:** Higher-level contract management features
:::

## Getting Started

To effectively use Allocated Contracts:

1. **Access the feature** via Management → Allocated Contracts
2. **Review your portfolio** using the Total and Active contract overview
3. **Browse contract cards** to see your assigned contracts
4. **Use search function** to find specific contracts quickly
5. **Click "View Details"** for comprehensive contract information
6. **Monitor contract status** and duration regularly
7. **Coordinate with teams** to ensure successful contract delivery

:::warning[Contract Responsibilities]
As a contract holder, you are responsible for ensuring successful delivery of services according to contract terms. Monitor progress regularly and escalate issues promptly to maintain client satisfaction and contract compliance.
:::
