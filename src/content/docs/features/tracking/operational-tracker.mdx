## What is the Operational Tracker?

The Operational Tracker provides comprehensive monitoring of operational activities with dual view modes:
- **Manager-focused tracking** - Monitor operational days by individual managers
- **Contract-focused tracking** - Track operational days by specific contracts
- **Monthly breakdowns** - Detailed month-by-month operational data
- **Performance analytics** - Total operational days and estimated projections

## Getting Started

To effectively use the Operational Tracker:

1. **Access the feature** via Overview → Operational Tracker
2. **Choose your view** between manager tracking and contract tracking
3. **Use filters** to focus on specific managers or contracts
4. **Review monthly data** to understand operational patterns
5. **Analyze totals** for overall performance assessment
6. **Compare estimates** with actual operational days (contracts view)
7. **Use insights** for operational planning and resource allocation

:::warning[Data Accuracy]
Operational tracking data should be reviewed regularly for accuracy. Ensure operational activities are properly recorded and assigned to maintain reliable tracking and planning information.
:::
