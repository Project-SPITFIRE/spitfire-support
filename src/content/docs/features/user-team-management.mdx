## What is user & team management?

User and team management encompasses:
- Creating and maintaining user accounts
- Organizing users into functional teams
- Assigning appropriate roles and permissions
- Managing access to system features and data
- Coordinating team structure with operational needs

## Permission management

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Role-Based Permissions">
    <p>
      <strong>Concept</strong>: Permissions assigned through roles, not individuals<br/>
      <strong>Benefits</strong>: Consistent access, easier management, clear boundaries<br/>
      <strong>Implementation</strong>: Users inherit all permissions from assigned role
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Data Access Controls">
    <p>
      <strong>Own Data</strong>: Users can view/edit their personal information<br/>
      <strong>Team Data</strong>: Managers can view their team's information<br/>
      <strong>System Data</strong>: Admins and Operations Support have broader access
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Feature Access">
    <p>
      <strong>Core Features</strong>: Available to all authenticated users<br/>
      <strong>Management Features</strong>: Restricted to management roles<br/>
      <strong>Administrative Features</strong>: Admin and Operations Support only
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

## User account statuses

### Active Statuses
- <Badge text="Active" variant="success" /> **Active**: Full access, normal operations
- <Badge text="Training" variant="caution" /> **Training**: Limited access during onboarding
- <Badge text="Pending Setup" variant="note" /> **Pending Setup**: Account created, awaiting configuration

### Inactive Statuses
- <Badge text="Suspended" variant="danger" /> **Suspended**: Temporarily disabled access
- <Badge text="Deactivated" variant="note" /> **Deactivated**: Permanently disabled, data retained
- <Badge text="Archived" variant="note" /> **Archived**: Historical record, no active access

## Best practices

**For Admins**:
- Follow principle of least privilege
- Regularly review user access and roles
- Document role changes and justifications
- Maintain up-to-date team structures
- Monitor for unused or orphaned accounts

**For Team Managers**:
- Keep team member information current
- Communicate access needs clearly
- Report role change requirements promptly
- Participate in regular access reviews
