## What is Lieu & Holidays?

The Lieu & Holidays system provides comprehensive tracking of:
- **Lieu Time** - Time off in lieu of overtime worked
- **Holiday Entitlements** - Annual leave and vacation days
- **Days Off Taken** - Actual time off used by staff
- **Balance Management** - Real-time tracking of available leave
- **Attendance Monitoring** - Monthly overview of staff attendance patterns

## Understanding Leave Types

<Accordion>

## Getting Started

To use the Lieu & Holidays tracker effectively:

1. **Access the feature** via Overview → Lieu & Holidays
2. **Select time period** using month and year dropdowns
3. **Search for staff** using the employee search function
4. **Review balances** across lieu time and holiday entitlements
5. **Monitor usage** through days off and holidays taken columns
6. **Track trends** by comparing different months and periods
7. **Report issues** if balances appear incorrect or outdated

:::warning[Data Accuracy]
Leave balance data should be reviewed regularly for accuracy. Report any discrepancies to Ops Support immediately to ensure staff have correct leave information for planning purposes.
:::
