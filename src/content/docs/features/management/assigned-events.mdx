## What are Assigned Events?

Assigned Events are operational events allocated to specific managers for planning, coordination, and execution. These include:
- **Sports Events** (Women's Super League, Pre-Season Friendlies)
- **Traffic Management Events** (TM 2 series)
- **Location-specific Events** across various venues
- **Succession-planned Events** with management coverage

## Best Practices

:::tip[Event Management Best Practices]
- **Regular Review:** Check assigned events regularly for updates and changes
- **Succession Coordination:** Maintain good communication with designated succession managers
- **Resource Planning:** Use event schedules for proactive resource planning
- **Multi-venue Management:** Plan carefully for events occurring on the same dates
- **Documentation:** Keep records of event execution and lessons learned
:::

:::note[Integration Points]
Assigned Events integrates with:
- **Team Management:** Assigning teams to specific events
- **Rota Management:** Scheduling staff for event operations
- **Allocated Contracts:** Events may be tied to specific contracts
- **EOS Actions:** Event-related tasks and coordination activities
:::

## Getting Started

To effectively use Assigned Events:

1. **Access the feature** via Management → Assigned Events
2. **Review your portfolio** using the Total Events overview
3. **Browse event history** to understand your assigned events
4. **Use search function** to find specific events quickly
5. **Review succession plans** to understand management coverage
6. **Check event locations** for coordination planning
7. **Plan resources** based on event schedules and requirements

:::tip[Event Management Responsibility]
As an assigned event manager, you are responsible for ensuring successful event execution. 
Coordinate with succession managers and teams to deliver high-quality operational support for all assigned events.
:::
