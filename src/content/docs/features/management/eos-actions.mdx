## What is an EOS Action?

EO tasks are structured work items that break down complex operational activities into manageable subtasks. They ensure coordinated execution across multiple team members with comprehensive tracking and progress monitoring.

## Task statuses

### Task-Level Statuses
- <Badge text="Not Started" variant="note" /> **Not Started**: Newly created, planning phase
- <Badge text="In Progress" variant="caution" /> **In Progress**: Active work on subtasks
- <Badge text="Completed" variant="success" /> **Completed**: All subtasks finished
- <Badge text="On Hold" variant="note" /> **On Hold**: Temporarily paused
- <Badge text="Cancelled" variant="danger" /> **Cancelled**: No longer required

### Subtask Statuses
- <Badge text="Not Started" variant="note" /> **Not Started**: Ready to begin
- <Badge text="In Progress" variant="caution" /> **In Progress**: Actively working
- <Badge text="Completed" variant="success" /> **Completed**: Finished and verified
- <Badge text="Blocked" variant="danger" /> **Blocked**: Cannot proceed due to dependency

## Best practices

**For Task Owners**:
- Break down complex work into clear, actionable subtasks
- Set realistic deadlines with buffer time
- Monitor progress regularly and update stakeholders
- Document important decisions and changes in task notes

**For Subtask Assignees**:
- Update status promptly when starting or completing work
- Communicate blockers immediately through task notes
- Ask questions rather than making assumptions
- Coordinate with other subtask owners when needed

**For Interface Usage**:
- Use filters to focus on relevant tasks
- Click tasks to access detailed information
- Regularly check activity logs for updates
- Utilize the notes feature for team communication

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Can't see my assigned tasks">
    <p>
      Check Management â†’ EOS Actions. Use the assignee filter to show only your tasks. Verify your role has task access. Contact Operations Support if tasks are missing.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Task not appearing in correct week">
    <p>
      Check the due date of the task. Tasks appear in weeks based on their due dates. Use the week filter to find tasks in different time periods.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Cannot update task status">
    <p>
      Verify you have permission to update the task. Check if you're the assigned user or have admin/ops support permissions. Contact task owner if status seems incorrect.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Subtask progress not updating">
    <p>
      Ensure subtasks are being marked as completed individually. The overall progress bar updates based on subtask completion ratios. Check subtask statuses in the detailed view.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
