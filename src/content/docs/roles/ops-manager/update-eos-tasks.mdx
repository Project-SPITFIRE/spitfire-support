You can update the status and details of EOS subtasks assigned to you within larger EOS tasks.

## Access your subtasks

1. Navigate to **Management** → **EOS Actions**
2. Find your task → Click to open **Task Details**
3. Locate the **Subtasks** section

## Update a subtask

1. Find your assigned subtask in the table
2. Click the subtask row or edit icon
3. Update status and add progress notes
4. Save changes

## Status options

<Badge text="Not Started" variant="note" /> Ready to begin work

<Badge text="In Progress" variant="caution" /> Actively working on this

<Badge text="Completed" variant="success" /> Fully finished and ready for review

## Best practices

- **Update weekly** or when significant progress occurs
- **Flag blockers immediately** - don't wait to report obstacles
- **Be specific** about what was accomplished
- **Coordinate** with other subtask assignees when needed

## What you can access

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="View Permissions">

    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Edit Permissions">
    <p>
      Your assigned subtasks' status only, task notes and comments, progress updates for your work.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>

:::note[Permissions]
You can only update subtasks specifically assigned to you. Contact the task owner or Ops Support for other modifications.
:::

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Can't update subtask">
    <p>
      Verify subtask is assigned to you, check if task is closed/archived, contact Ops Support if access issues persist.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Unclear requirements">
    <p>
      Review task Description section, check existing notes for clarifications, add note requesting clarification, contact task creator or Ops Support.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Conflicting priorities">
    <p>
      Document conflict in task notes, contact Ops Support to prioritize, request timeline adjustment if needed.
    </p>
  </AccordionItem>
